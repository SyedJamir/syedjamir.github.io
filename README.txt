Add your own images here:

- logo.png   -> a small square logo (used for the nav mark, if you enable it)
- profile.jpg -> your photo, referenced in about.html

The original design didn't use a photo, so about.html points to
images/profile.jpg as a placeholder. Drop your photo in with that
exact filename and it will show up automatically. If you don't want
a photo, just delete the <img> tag in about.html.
